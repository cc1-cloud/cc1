List of farms. Each farm is described by:

  • Name,
  • Creation time and uptime,
  • Templates (hardware parameters) of head and of worker nodes,
  • Head public and private IP,
  • VNC access details,
  • Disks attached to it.

Actions you can perform on farm depend on it's state. If farm's state is other than running, some operations are unavailable. Farm that is failed needs to be removed by administrator.
