ISO images have a lot in commont with standard Images. Those can store operating system and may be bootable. One can upload them and subsequently run VM with ISO attached. Such machine may boot from that ISO image provided it is bootable.

On the other hand those are immutable, therefore can't be modified and saved back to user's pool.
