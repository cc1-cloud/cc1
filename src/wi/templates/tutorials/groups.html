Group is a users unit that allowes sharing images between its members.

In Show groups section you'll find the list of all active groups. To join an existing group you must click send request at chosen group, which will notify the group leader. Once request is accepted, you will gain access to the group resources. In My groups section you can create new groups and manage ones that belong to you.

Be aware that any private keys and other data you left on the image you share become available for all group members.

It's strongly recommended to remove all unnecessary private keys and ensure security.
