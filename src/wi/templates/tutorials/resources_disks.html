Here you can manage your virtual disks.

Disks are intended for storage. You need one if you are going to collect a lot of data.

You can create one that takes size according to your needs. Such a disk may be further attached to a virtual machine.

Note, that only disks with USB or virtio bus may be attached to and detached from running VMs. Other disks need to be attached the moment VM is being created.

Available operations:

  • Add new disk,
  • Edit existing disk,
  • Remove existing disk.


Disks are not images. Images are dedicated to keep virtual machine's configuration.
