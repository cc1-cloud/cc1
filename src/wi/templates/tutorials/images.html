Here you can browse, upload and manage virtual machine images.

Images are dedicated to keep virtual machine's configuration, such as operating system, software and configs. Thats what new virtual machines are created from and existing ones are saved to.

All images are divided into 3 groups:

  • Public images - images available for everyone.
  • My images - your images, nobody else can see or use them.
  • Group images - your groups' images, available to your groups' members.

Disks on the other hand are intended to store your data.
